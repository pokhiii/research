\begin{abstract}
In 2017, eight researchers at Google published a fifteen-page paper that, in hindsight, changed the trajectory of artificial intelligence.
The paper introduced the Transformer, and if you have used ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or really any AI system built after 2018, you have used a descendant of what they described.
This series takes that paper apart, idea by idea, and explains it in a way that assumes you are intelligent but not necessarily a machine learning researcher.
Part 1 covers just the abstract --- which turns out to be a surprisingly dense six sentences.
\end{abstract}

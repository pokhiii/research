\begin{abstract}
Deep learning has quietly become the backbone of modern technology --- from the camera that recognizes your face to the assistant that writes your emails.
Yet many people assume that with the rise of ChatGPT and large language models, deep learning itself is somehow old news.
This paper tells a different story.
We trace the arc of deep learning from its earliest ideas in the 1950s, through its dramatic comeback in the 2010s, to the 2017 breakthrough that changed everything, and all the way to the models making headlines today.
The goal is simple: make this story accessible to anyone curious enough to read it.
\end{abstract}
